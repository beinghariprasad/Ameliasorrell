Questions parents ask

The honest answers.

What ages is this for?
Ages 0–10. The Blueprint splits into three age-specific sections (0–3, 4–6, 7–10), so you can jump straight to where your child is. If you have more than one kid in the range, you'll use all three.
Is this a parenting course?
No. There are no videos, no modules, no log-ins. It's an 80+ page PDF you download once and keep forever. Read it on your phone, print the worksheets, mark it up. It's yours.
What if my child is just… normal?
Every child has a way of being curious that's specifically theirs. "Normal" isn't the opposite of interesting — it's the starting point. The Blueprint helps you see the texture inside the everyday.
I work full-time. Will I have time for this?
Yes. The 30-day plan is built around 5–15 minute prompts. Most of the framework is about noticing what's already happening, not adding new activities to your week.
Do I need to buy supplies?
Almost never. The Talent Stations use things you already own — a kitchen drawer, a stack of paper, a flashlight. There's a budget-friendly list for the few times you might want to add something.
